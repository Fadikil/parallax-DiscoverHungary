Discover Hungary

Budapest

The capital of Hungary located along the Danube River. Known for the Parliament, Buda Castle and historic bridges connecting Buda and Pest.

Lake Balaton

The largest lake in Central Europe and Hungary's most popular summer destination with beaches, sailing and beautiful sunset views.

Eger

A historic town famous for its castle, thermal baths and the Egri Bikavér red wine.

Hortobágy National Park

The largest natural grassland in Europe and a UNESCO heritage site known for wildlife and traditional Hungarian shepherd culture.

Pécs

A historic university city filled with Roman ruins, Ottoman heritage and vibrant culture in southern Hungary.
